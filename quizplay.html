Quiz — How to Play

Fast, fair and fun — read the rules before you start.

⏱️ 60s 12 Qs 🟡 10 / correct

Main Rules

  1. 60 seconds total to answer all 12 questions.
  2. Each question has 4 options, only one correct.
  3. 10 coins for each correct answer; no negatives.
  4. Auto next when an answer is selected.
  5. Timer runs nonstop — finish before it ends.
  6. Can end early; score counts from answered ones.

Fair Play

  • No cheating or multiple accounts.
  • Questions shuffled for fairness.
  • Disconnected games count only answered questions.
  • Ties resolved by fastest total answer time.

Tips

  • Answer quickly — trust your first instinct.
  • Skim options; pick what stands out.
  • Practice to improve speed and accuracy.

Max possible coins: 120
